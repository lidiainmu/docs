[Sanity](https://www.sanity.io/) is a [headless CMS](https://www.sanity.io/headless-cms) that uses [structured content](https://www.sanity.io/structured-content) to endlessly re-use content across any channel and a composable approach to help businesses connect to any third-party technology, data source, and front end framework.

## Official Resources

- [Build your blog with Astro and Sanity](https://www.sanity.io/guides/sanity-astro-blog) - A complete guide to setting up your blog using Astro and Sanity.

- [Clean Sanity + Astro template](https://www.sanity.io/templates/astro-sanity-clean) - A minimal Astro site with Sanity Studio.

## Community Resources 

- Use the [`astro-sanity` integration](https://github.com/littlesticks/astro-sanity) to add Sanity to your project.

- You can get started with the [Astro Sanity Minimal Starter](https://github.com/littlesticks/astro-sanity-minimal-starter).

- Video: [Create your own Blog with Astro and Sanity.io](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-jAWLTfsSQw) by Jaydan Urwin
